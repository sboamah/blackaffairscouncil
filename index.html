Our Mission

The Black Affairs Council aims to build a sense and represent the entirety of the black community with students, administrative staff  and various affiliated organizations within Stevens Institute of Technology. Using the collaborative efforts of all of the Black leaders on campus and administrative staff, we hope to give those that identify with the black race opportunities to voice their opinions, develop educational opportunities, enhance cultural awareness, and provide support to other under-resourced communities outside of Stevens.

Organizations

In Black Student Union (BSU) at Stevens Institute of Technology, we are all unified in one voice in order to embrace and enrich African-American culture on this campus.

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) is the largest student run organization in the world! NSBE has three objectives for its members: to excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community.

The Phi Theta Chapter of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc. was officially chartered on January 12th, 2019 by 5 prestigious gentlemen aiming to solidify a stronger African-American presence on the campus of Stevens Institute of Technology and the surrounding communities of Hoboken & Jersey City.

The African Student Association at Stevens institute of technology is a graduate run organization that is centered around the inclusivity of african students, whether it is undergraduate or graduate.
