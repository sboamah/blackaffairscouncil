Excel, Succeed, and Impact

Mission Statement

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) is the largest student run organization in the world! NSBE has three objectives for its members: to excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community. This year the chapter has many events planned that to fulfill this mission. We will be implementing study and mentor groups, we will be hosting an etiquette dinner and exposing high school students in Hoboken to engineering.
