Mission Statement

In Black Student Union (BSU) at Stevens Institute of Technology, we are all unified in one voice in order to embrace and enrich African-American culture on this campus. Everyone is welcomed, inclusive; our passions and ambitions drive us so that we can make a difference. We can be heard, appreciated, and respected for the work we do, have done, and are going to do. We all learn from each other and our surroundings, make mistakes so we may teach those that seek to join our discussions that all we want is to be heard.
